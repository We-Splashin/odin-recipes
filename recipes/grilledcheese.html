Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese sandwiches are the best thing ever

Serve with a bowl of tomato soup

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Assemble a cheese sandwich by putting a slice of cheese between two pieces of bread
  2. Scrap a small amount of butter on each side of the cheese sandwich
  3. Place the sandwich on a hot pan
  4. Flip when the first side is golden brown
  5. Turn off heat when other side is golden brown and remove grilled cheese from pan
