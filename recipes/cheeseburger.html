Cheese Burger

Description

Cheese burgers are my favorite food to make

Burgers are best made on a grill so this recipe will go into

how to grill a burger.

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Ground Beef

    • Optional:

  • American Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties
  2. Season ground beef patties
  3. Throw the patties on a piping hot grill
  4. When moisture starts to pool on top of the patties flip them over
  5. Optionally add cheese
  6. Take patties off grill and place on burger bun
  7. Add any other optional ingredients
