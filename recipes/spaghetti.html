Spaghetti

My wife makes really good spaghetti.

It's her favorite dish so she makes it all the time.

I dont mind. ;)

Description

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Fill pot with water and some salt
  2. Boil pot of water
  3. Add Spaghetti Noodles to boiling pot of water
  4. Stir occasionally
  5. Noodles are done when they start sticking
  6. Strain water out of noodle pot
  7. Brown Ground Beef in a pan
  8. Add Spaghetti Sauce to pan with beef
  9. Season Sauce to taste
  10. Plate by putting noodles to a plate and pour sauce on top
